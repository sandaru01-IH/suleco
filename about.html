Dr.Surantha Salgadoe

Crop Remote Sensing Specialist and Precision Agriculturist

PhD, UNE, Australia / Senior Lecturer at Faculty of Agriculture and Plantation Management, Wayamba University of Sri Lanka and Founder of AGCEYLON HUB His research interests include crop remote sensing, precision agriculture, plant pathology, and sustainable agricultural practices.
He has collaborated both locally and internationally, contributing significantly to the advancement of agricultural technology in Sri Lanka. His work has been recognized with several awards.

Dr. Milinda Piyasena

Senior Lecturer Dep. of Surveying and Geodesy Faculty of Geomatics Sabaragamuwa University.

Dr. Piyasena specializes in land tenure security, slum upgrading, and agent-based simulation in land management. He has published extensively on land tenure reform and spatial data infrastructure.
As Lead Researcher of the Rs. 30 million AHEAD-funded InfoBhoomi Project, he has advanced geospatial intelligence using GIS, remote sensing, and decision support systems.
